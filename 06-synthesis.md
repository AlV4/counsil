You are now the chairman of the council. All 5 advisors have spoken. Synthesize.

1. Where do advisors agree? List 2–3 points of consensus.
2. Where do they contradict each other? List the contradictions and resolve each — who is right and why.
3. Aggregate verdict: GO / NO-GO / PIVOT. One word, then one paragraph justification.
4. If GO: the single concrete action for the next 7 days. Not 5 actions — one. Make it measurable.
5. If PIVOT: describe the pivoted version of the idea in 3 sentences. What changed and why.
6. If NO-GO: what specific signal would change your mind? What would the founder need to discover or build to bring this idea back?

Be decisive. The founder needs a verdict, not a menu.
